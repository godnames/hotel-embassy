A Warm Welcome Awaits at Hotel Embassy Park

Hotel Embassy Park is a budget hotel located in Santacruz East, Mumbai. It offers basic yet comfortable accommodations at affordable rates for both business and leisure travelers.

The 38 well-appointed rooms at Hotel Embassy Park feature air-conditioning, LED TV's, wi-fi access and attached bathrooms with hot water supply. Select from Deluxe Twin Rooms, Deluxe Double Rooms, Superior Double Rooms, Superior Triple Rooms categories based on your requirements. The rooms are clean, spacious and designed to ensure a relaxing stay.
